## Money-Saving Strategies

### Pre-Trip Savings
1. **Book 8-12 weeks early** - Best prices
2. **Set price alerts** - Save $50-150
3. **Be flexible on dates** - ±7 days = big savings
4. **Travel shoulder season** - May, November

### On-Island Savings
1. **Eat local** - 70% cheaper than resort
2. **Free activities** - Snorkel, walks, beach
3. **No paid tours** - First days just explore
4. **Share activities** - Split costs with others

### Packing Smart
- Download offline maps (save data)
- Bring reef-safe sunscreen (pricey there)
- Basic medications (expensive at resort)
- Snacks from home
