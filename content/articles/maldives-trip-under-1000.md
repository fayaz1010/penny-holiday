# Maldives Trip Under $1,000 – Complete Plan

**7 Nights for Under $1,000/Person**

## Reality Check
Yes, it's possible with ultra-budget travel:
- Ultra-cheap flight: $450-600
- Budget resort: $350-500 (7 nights)
- Food/activities: $100-150
- **Total: $900-1,250**

## Flight Hacks ($450-600)
- Book 3+ months early
- Fly mid-week (Tue-Wed)
- Accept long stopovers
- Consider budget airlines

## Cheapest Resorts ($50-70/night)
During monsoon season (May-Oct):
- Embudu: $50-80
- Holiday Island: $60-90
- Basic but functional

## Daily Budget ($20)
- Breakfast: $2 (cafe)
- Lunch: $8 (local)
- Dinner: $10 (restaurant)
- Total: $20/day

## What to Skip
- Paid tours
- Alcohol
- Spa treatments
- Premium activities

## Tips
✅ Travel monsoon season
✅ Negotiate at resort
✅ Eat local always
✅ Free snorkeling only
