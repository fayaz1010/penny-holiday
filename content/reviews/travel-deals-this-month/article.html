FAQ: Booking Deals This Month

Q: When's the best time to book this month?

A: For late May/early June travel: book now. For July-August: watch prices; might drop in week 2-3.

Q: Are last-minute deals always cheaper?

A: Not always. Last-minute deals work for specific routes/hotels when airlines need to fill seats. General rule: 6-8 weeks ahead cheaper.

Q: Should I use a travel agent for deals?

A: Only if they specialize in travel deals. Most add 10-15% markup. Self-booking typically cheaper.

Q: What if the price drops after I book?

A: Most airlines/hotels offer 24-48 hour cancellation for free. Rebook if price drops, cancel original.

Q: Best month to book for next year?

A: September/October for next June-August travels (often 25-35% cheaper if booking in advance).
