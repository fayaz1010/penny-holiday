Money-Saving Strategies

Pre-Trip Savings

  • Book 8-12 weeks early - Best prices
  • Set price alerts - Save $50-150
  • Be flexible on dates - ±7 days = big savings
  • Travel shoulder season - May, November

On-Island Savings

  • Eat local - 70% cheaper than resort
  • Free activities - Snorkel, walks, beach
  • No paid tours - First days just explore
  • Share activities - Split costs with others

Packing Smart

  • Download offline maps (save data)
  • Bring reef-safe sunscreen (pricey there)
  • Basic medications (expensive at resort)
  • Snacks from home
